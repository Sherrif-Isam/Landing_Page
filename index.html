Our Treatments

Indulge in a blissful escape with our comprehensive selection of spa treatments, meticulously crafted to rejuvenate the body, soothe the soul, and restore inner harmony, ensuring a transformative experience that leaves you feeling pampered, refreshed, and radiant from head to toe.

Sphinx Stone Therapy

Unwind and rejuvenate with our Sphinx Stone Therapy, where heated stones are expertly placed and massaged on your body, melting away tension and promoting deep relaxation.

Cleopatra's Milk Bath

Channel the timeless beauty of Cleopatra with our luxurious Milk Bath, enriched with nourishing ingredients to pamper your skin and evoke a sense of ancient royalty.

Amani Rinas Henna
